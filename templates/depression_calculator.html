Why Use the Depression Calculator?

Our Depression Calculator is designed to provide you with insights into your mental well-being. By analyzing various factors including age, gender, and psychological symptoms, it offers an assessment of your potential risk for depression.

Early detection of depression symptoms can lead to timely intervention and treatment, improving overall mental health outcomes.
